1. 首页（Tab 1）UI设计提示词
页面定位：平台核心流量入口与教练曝光场，聚焦“找私教”。

Prompt:
UI design for a fitness mini-program homepage, named "Lianyu Fitness". Style: Warm, professional, and social, strictly differentiating from Keep's cold, minimalist style. Color Palette: High-saturation vital Warm Orange (#FF7D3B) as accent color, Deep Space Gray (#2A2D34) for text, against a warm white background, with no large areas of cold colors. Layout (top to bottom): A clean search bar with a message center icon. A banner carousel showing high-quality, real-life warm photos of professional coaches teaching students (not illustrations). A core function grid area with large, friendly icons for "Smart Coach Matching" (highlighted with warm orange), "Paid Membership", and "City Fitness Buddy/Group Class". Below, a horizontal scrolling list of "Nearby Gold Coaches" presented in large, soft-shadowed cards (20px rounded corners) featuring coach photos, ratings, and a distinct Warm Orange "Book Trial" button. Minimalist, breathable typography with ample whitespace. The overall feeling is trustworthy and inviting.
